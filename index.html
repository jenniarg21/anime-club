Welcome!

This is the web site for the Anime Club at NOVA, Alexandria Campus. We meet every Thursday in the Bisdorf Building at 3:30pm.

Activites

Here, at the Anime Club, we do many activites. Below are the activities we try to do at every meeting.

  • Anime
  • Manga Drawing
  • Martial Arts

Anime

As would be figured by the name of the Club, we watch anime. We try to watch at least two different anime episodes at every meeting so all the memebers can get a grasp of what type of animes there are. And we also have an Anime we watch reoccuringly.

The animes we watch are nominated by memebers of the club and voted at the end of the meeting to see what animes we will watch the meeting after.

Manga Drawing

To give our memebers a try at drawing what we like to watch, our stand-in President holds lessons after every meeting. Mini-lessons are also handed out for members reference.

Our goal as a Club is to start our own Manga (comic book) once we've mastered our drawing lessons. However, if you are a new member and wish to learn as well, do not fret and feel as though you might be behind in the lessons. The more our members learn the more teachers there will be for our new members.

Martial Arts

Our stand-in President teaches us Wado-ryu after the drawing lessons. Wado-ryu is a form of martial arts that originated in Japan. It deals with using your opponents strength against them and not exerting any of your own strength. It is taught to use this form of martial arts as self defense. In the events gallery you will find a few random photos of us learning.
